About Us

At QRCodeHQ, we aim to help restaurant owners and businesses be able to
convert their important documents to a simple QR Code for their customers.
We saw that during these times with COVID-19, re-used physical documents
such as menus could spread germs between the people that touch them,
thus we decided to create a simple, and efficient tool to make lives easier.



If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact us at: support@qrcodehq.com
